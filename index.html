About Me

Hi! My name is Akash Patney, and I am currently a student at the University of Michigan majoring in computer science engineering. As of now, my goal is to pursue a career in software engineering.

In the past two summers, I have done software engineering internships at Ford Motor Company. Most recently, I worked as a software engineer intern in the Supply Chain group where we were responsible for managing and processing data for Ford's suppliers. I gained experience with a variety of tools including several of GCP's Big Data cloud services (Google Dataflow, Google Pub/Sub, Google Big Query, etc.), Apache Beam paired with Java, and several deployement and configuration tools such as Terraform, Tekton, and YAML. The primary project I worked on was related to taking the data aggregation pipelines that currently existed for supplier data and replacing them with a single Google Data Flow Job. This replacement offered numerous advantages over the previous structure of the pipelines including a consolidation of the logic for the data aggregation, lower runtimes and costs, the ability to version control, and the ability to test the code.

In the summer before the most recent, I worked as a full stack software engineer intern at Ford Motor Company for the Dealer Information System Net Enabled team (DISNE). During this internship I worked with a variety of both front and backend technologies, including Java, Spring, Angular, DB2, Jenkins, and PCF. The primary goal of the developers on this team was to redesign a web application called DISNE, which was used to store information for car dealerships that Ford worked with. This was a positive experience for me, since it allowed me to see what it is like to work as a softare engineer in a professional environment for the first time. Gaining exposure to the common software engineering practices and applying the skills I have learned from school was really beneficial, and I hope to continue to build and develop my computer science skills.

To gain experience applying some of the skills learned from school and to learn new ones, I have also dedicated time towards creating personal projects. I have highlighted some of these personal projects on this website. Additionally, my github contains the project code for many of these projects.
